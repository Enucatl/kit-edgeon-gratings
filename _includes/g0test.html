Experimental setup

The interferometer is designed for a pi shift at 120 keV and the first Talbot order. G1 and G2 are the same throughout the tests for all the G0s and were also made by Microworks.

The tube is a Comet MXR/160-HP11 with a voltage of 160 kVp and a current of 10 mA.

The detector is a Mythen module also illuminated in an edge-on fashion, with a minimum energy threshold of 20 keV.
