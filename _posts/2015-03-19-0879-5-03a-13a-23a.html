Experimental setup

The interferometer is designed for a pi shift at 120 keV and the third Talbot order.

The tube is a Comet MXR/160-HP11 with a voltage of 160 kVp and a current of 10 mA.

The detector is a Pilatus CdTe module with a minimum energy threshold of 20 keV.
